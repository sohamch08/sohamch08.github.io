Hi, I am Soham Chatterjee.

I completed my B.Sc. in Mathematics and Computer Science at Chennai Mathematical Institute (2021-2024). I am also an alumnus of Ramakrishna Mission, Baranagar and Narendranath Vidyamandir where i completed my Secondary (10th Standard) and Higher secondary (12th Standard) education.

Theoretical Computer Science is where my interests lie mostly. I am brodly interested in:

  • Algorithms and Complexity Theory with a pinch of algebraic flavour
  • Error Correcting Codes
  • Pseudorandomness
  • Polyhedral Combinatorics
  • Property Testing of Boolean Functions
  • Quantum Computing and Quantum Information Theory

If you're interested, you may see my blog on complexity theory here.

During my Bachelors I have done research internships on topics like Algebraic Complexity Theory, Property Testing of Boolean Functions and their Quantum Algorithms, Polyhedral Combinatorics. You can check my page on Research Internships and Talks for more details and their reports and slides of my presentations. You can also visit my page on Course Work to get all lecture notes of the courses I have taken or been taking.

Besides course work I love to typeset my notes $\LaTeX$. It is a great hobby of mine to make my documents beautiful in $\LaTeX$. I've been using it for more than 5 years. I have created a theme with which i typeset my lecture notes and reports.
